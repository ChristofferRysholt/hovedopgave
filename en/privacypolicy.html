Personal information is any kind of information that can be attributed to your person in different ways. By using our contact form at skotteknive.dk, we collect and process this information.

To the extent that you yourself explicitly consent to this and enter the information yourself are processed: Name, telephone number, e-mail, address.

The primary area from which we collect information is on our contact form. If you use this, we will have the information you enter with yours CONSENT.

Security

We have taken technical and professional measures to prevent your information being accidentally or illegally deleted, published, lost, impaired or in the knowledge of unauthorized persons, abused or otherwise treated in violation of the law.

Purpose

The information is used for two main purposes:

  • To contact you either by mail or phone, upon request
  • To transfer your information to our customer database when purchasing products from Skotteknive.dk.

In addition, we do NOT currently use a third party to store and process data.

Disclosure of personal information such as name and email etc. will only happen if you consent to it.

Storing Period

We will keep personal information about you for as long as we need to fulfill the stated purposes. However, personal data will be deleted if there is no further contact within 18 months. Storage is in accordance with the rules on bookkeeping and the limitation of criminal liability (absolute deadlines), if applicable. If it is not relevant, we delete the personal information beforehand.

The minimum period for retention under the Posting Act is p.t. current calendar year and 5 years.

Information requirements

It is up to you to ensure that the personal data you provide is accurate and complete. Please do not provide personal sensitive information such as religion, sexual orientation, race or health information.

Please note that your email address acts as your unique ID when sending your email information. We therefore recommend that you enter a personal email address that others do not have access to.

Your rightsr

You have some rights in our processing of your contact information because it is to a very large extent electronic:

  • You have the right to know that we have collected information about you and stored it in our IT system
  • You have the right to know what information about you we have collected and used in our processing of your application and you have the right to see the information
  • You have the right to ask that we correct or delete information if you believe they are, for example, incorrect or give a wrong impression. And we have a duty to consider your request.

You can read more at Datatilsynets website www.datatilsynet.dk
