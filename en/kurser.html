Course in knife making

Due to my work with the knives I have been given the opportunity to teach. I can therefore offer lessons to associations, scouts or a bunch of friends with the desire to learn how to make knives. It takes approx. 20-25 hours for beginner to make a knife as well as a leather sheath for the knife.

I have experience teaching hunting associations, tree twins and people with an interest in knives, in all age groups. Then you stand and lack an instructor, you are welcome to contact me.

Courses in the knife workshop

I do a little more intimate courses in knife making than most, where I indulge the participants with my knowledge, delicious materials, good craftsmanship and bright and lovely surroundings at the workshop.

The courses are from 3-6 participants max as quality of teaching is a high priority.

I hold private, scouting and purification courses, including hunting clubs. A weekend-end course costs 1200 DKK per participant plus materials.
