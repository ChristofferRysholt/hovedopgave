About Stuart Harvey

My name is Stuart Harvey, I am 52 years old and born in Paisley - Scotland. I moved to Denmark in 1989 and am a trained refrigerator engineer as well as medical technician. I live at Mols and have 2 lovely boys (Christopher and Alexander).

The whole adventure of making hunting knives started in 2001 when I got my lovely black labrador Thor. I went to dog training with him and soon found out how good a hunting dog he was. That led to me getting hunting signs in 2003.

n the year 2004, the knives came into the picture when I was going to use a hunting knife and of course I wanted to make it myself. For the next four years I attended a knife course at Michael West and I have also been on 2 courses with Jørgen Spotteck and Ib Dyhr - all three of Denmark's most skilled knife makers.

Handmade Special Knives

It has gradually become a production of 300 knives in the kitchen, hunting and fishing. I might consider to extend the range to mushroom knives as well, but does not currently have. experience with this type of knives.

I am passionate about my craft and challenge myself to think in new ways, in relation to material and now also to expand the range for mushroom knives. I think it's exciting to make the knives and look forward to your inquiry - maybe the next knife is yours?
